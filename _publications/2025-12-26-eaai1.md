This paper introduces an image-enhanced generative model for industrial scanning acoustic super-resolution in non-destructive testing. The method combines generative modeling with image enhancement to improve acoustic image resolution and inspection reliability.

## Abstract
We propose a novel generative framework for scanning acoustic microscopy that enhances acoustic image quality and resolution using image-based guidance. Our approach improves defect detection performance in industrial non-destructive testing while preserving the fine structural details of acoustic images.

## Key Contributions
- Developed an image-enhanced generative model targeted at acoustic super-resolution
- Demonstrated improved resolution and inspection quality for industrial scanning acoustic data
- Validated the method in non-destructive testing scenarios with practical relevance
