Presented the ICIT 2025 paper on SAMTransGAN, a Transformer-based super-resolution framework for high-resolution scanning acoustic imaging in industrial nondestructive testing.

<a href="https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/10965207" class="btn" target="_blank">IEEE Xplore</a>

## Abstract

This presentation covered the development of SAMTransGAN, a GAN-plus-Transformer architecture for acoustic image super-resolution. It included dataset generation via a high-order degradation model, transfer learning, dynamic loss balancing, and a web-based deployment framework for industrial NDT applications.
